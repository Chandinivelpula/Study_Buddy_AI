What is StudyBuddy AI?

Your notes, supercharged by AI

StudyBuddy AI is a web application built for students who want to spend less time rewriting and more time actually learning. Take a photo of your handwritten notes and our AI handles the rest.

Upload any image of handwritten notes — messy, quick, or multi-page
Google Gemini reads and transcribes your handwriting into clean, editable text
An integrated AI tutor can summarize, quiz you, explain topics, and create flashcards instantly
No complex setup — it just works in your browser

Problem & Solution

Why StudyBuddy AI exists

⚠️ The Problem

Notes are a mess

  • ✕
    Handwritten notes are often illegible, unorganized, and hard to search through later.
  • ✕
    Revising from raw handwriting is slow and mentally exhausting.
  • ✕
    Students waste hours rewriting notes digitally just to make them usable.
  • ✕
    Creating quizzes and flashcards from scratch takes more time than actually studying.
✓ The Solution

AI does the heavy lifting

  • ✓
    Instant digitization — upload once, get clean text in seconds with no manual typing.
  • ✓
    AI-generated summaries let you review entire chapters in minutes.
  • ✓
    Auto-generated quizzes and flashcards make active recall effortless.
  • ✓
    A built-in AI tutor answers your questions without leaving the app.
